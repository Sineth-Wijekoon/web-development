735M PEOPLE FACE WORLD HUNGER
Food exists, but waste & inequality persist.
Many hungry people grow food but can’t afford it.
Hunger kills more than AIDS, malaria & TB.
There is more than enough food produced in the world to feed everyone on the planet. Yet 733 million people still go hungry.

Our Mission

We are committed to ending hunger and promoting sustainable agriculture in line with the UN’s Sustainable Development Goal 2 by 2030. Through innovation, education, and collaboration, we empower communities to adopt responsible food practices that nourish people and protect the planet.
